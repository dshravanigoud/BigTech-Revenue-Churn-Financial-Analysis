## Output 1 — Quarterly Financial Overview

Tracks revenue, EBITDA, net income, EPS, and free cash flow across 9 quarters (Q1 2021 – Q1 2023), with QoQ growth rates and margin analysis.

![Quarterly Financial Overview](output1_quarterly_financials.png)

**Highlights:**
- Revenue grew steadily from **$501M (Q1 2021)** to **$571M (Q1 2023)**
- EBITDA margin ranged from **7% (Q4)** to **27% (Q1)** — strong Q1 seasonality
- FCF turned significantly positive in Q2 2022 (+$56M) after several negative quarters
- Net Income per Share recovered to **$3.27** in Q1 2023 after crashing to **$0.12** in Q4 2022

---

## Output 2 — Customer Trends & Churn Analysis

Models subscriber lifecycle — new users, attrition, net change, and churn rate — on a quarterly basis.

![Customer Trends & Churn](output2_customer_trends.png)

**Highlights:**
- Churn spiked dramatically in **Q1–Q2 2022** (14.9% and 36.4%) following a price increase from **$33 → $34/quarter**
- User base dropped from **16,409 (Q2 2022 start)** to a low of **11,620** by end of Q2 2022
- Recovery was strong — users rebounded to **17,118** by end of Q1 2023
- Net new users of **637K** in Q1 2023 signal stabilization post-churn crisis

## Output 3 — Annual Projections & CAGR

Forward-looking model projecting FY2023E and FY2024E financials, with a 2020–2024E CAGR summary.

![Annual Projections](output3_annual_projections.png)

**Highlights:**
- Revenue projected to reach **$2.63B by FY2024E**, up 15% YoY
- EBITDA expected to recover to **$528M** (20% margin) in FY2024E
- FCF trajectory turns strongly positive: **$93M (2023E)** → **$95M (2024E)**
- Debt/EBITDA leverage declining from **10.1x (2022)** to **7.4x (2024E)** — improving balance sheet health

## 💡 Key Insights

1. **Price Sensitivity is Real** — The $1/quarter price increase in Q1 2022 triggered a churn crisis that wiped out ~5,000K users over two quarters. Revenue held up due to higher per-user pricing, but the user base took 3 quarters to recover.

2. **Q4 is Structurally Weak** — EBITDA and Net Income collapsed every Q4 (8% and 7% margins), suggesting heavy year-end spend or seasonality effects that require further investigation.

3. **FCF Volatility** — Free cash flow swung from -$40M to +$56M within two quarters, indicating lumpy capex or working capital cycles that smooth out on an annual basis.

4. **Recovery Trajectory is Healthy** — FY2024E projections show improving margins, declining leverage, and positive FCF — pointing to operational stabilization post-churn event.
